India is one of the biggest producers of agricultural products and still has very less farm productivity. Productivity needs to be increased so that farmers can get more pay from the same piece of land with less labour. Precision agriculture provides a way to do it. Precision farming , as the name implies, refers to the applying of precise and proper total of comment like pee , fertilizers, soil etc. at the proper time to the craw for increasing its productivity and increasing its yields.

We provide our users a recommendation algorithm, which recommends them a crop which is suitable for their soil.
